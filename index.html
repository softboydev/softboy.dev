I specialise in minimal user interfaces and interactive experiences that rely on modern web technologies and a unique digital design language.

After having worked as a freelance webdesigner and developer for 3 years I started working at a medium-sized agency in Hamburg.

There I developed complex frontends for marketing agencies and brands such as Evonik, Camel or Takeda.

I also developed a custom solution to display the lifes work of German artist Ralf Ziervogel on a singular website before I took on the development of a biometric scanning app for the American start-up Zellerfeld Shoes.

When my application to HFBK got accepted I decided to quit agency work for now and focus on further developing my artistic language.
